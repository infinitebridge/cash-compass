# Cash Compass MVP: Comprehensive Financial Management Platform

## Overview

FinanceFlow MVP is a streamlined financial management platform designed for small and medium businesses with a single admin role. The platform encompasses four core modules: Expense Tracking, Revenue Management, Budgeting, and Reporting - providing a complete financial management solution in a simplified interface.

## Key Modules & Features

### 1. Dashboard & Navigation

**Purpose**: Provide a central command center for all financial activities.

**Key Features**:

- Financial snapshot with current month stats
- Expense vs. Revenue comparison chart
- Cash position indicator and trend
- Quick navigation to key functions
- Notice center for urgent financial matters

### 2. Expense Tracking

**Purpose**: Monitor, record, and analyze all business expenditures.

**Key Features**:

- Expense entry with categorization
- Receipt capture and storage
- Expense listing with advanced filtering
- Basic approval tracking (single-user)
- Vendor management

### 3. Revenue Management

**Purpose**: Track, analyze, and forecast all income streams.

**Key Features**:

- Revenue entry with source tracking
- Customer management
- Revenue categorization
- Payment method tracking
- Revenue trend analysis

### 4. Invoice Management

**Purpose**: Create, send, and manage customer invoices.

**Key Features**:

- Invoice creation from revenue entries
- Invoice template customization
- Payment status tracking
- Payment reminder capabilities
- Invoice export (PDF)

### 5. Budgeting

**Purpose**: Plan and monitor financial targets.

**Key Features**:

- Basic budget creation by category
- Budget vs. actual comparison
- Visual indicators for over/under budget
- Monthly budget projections

### 6. Reporting

**Purpose**: Generate insights from financial data.

**Key Features**:

- Pre-configured report templates
- Date range selection
- Export capabilities (CSV, PDF)
- Basic visualization options
- Tax-ready reports
