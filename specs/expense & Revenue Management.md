## Expense & Revenue Management Implementation

### Expense Tracking Workflow

**1. Expense Creation**

The expense tracking system allows you to document every business expenditure through:

- **Manual Entry**: Record expenses with these key fields:

  - Date
  - Amount
  - Category (from predefined list)
  - Vendor
  - Description
  - Payment method
  - Project/Client allocation (optional)
  - Tax deductible status

- **Receipt Handling**:

  - Upload images directly from device
  - Capture with mobile camera
  - Store digital copies for reference
  - Simple OCR to extract basic data

- **Bulk Import**: Upload expense data from CSV for efficiency

**2. Expense Categorization**

Proper categorization is critical for financial analysis:

- **Category Structure**:

  - Two-level hierarchy (main category, subcategory)
  - Customizable categories with preset defaults
  - Tax-relevant tagging
  - Project/client allocation

- **Common Categories**:

  - Office Supplies
  - Travel & Accommodation
  - Software & Subscriptions
  - Marketing & Advertising
  - Professional Services
  - Rent & Utilities
  - Equipment & Maintenance

- **Auto-Categorization**:
  - Pattern recognition based on vendor
  - Suggestions from previous similar expenses

**3. Expense Analysis**

Extract meaningful insights from expense data:

- **Filtering & Search**:

  - Date range (This month, Last month, Custom)
  - Category
  - Amount range
  - Vendor
  - Payment method
  - Tax status

- **Visualizations**:

  - Category breakdown (pie/donut chart)
  - Monthly trend (line/bar chart)
  - Top spending areas
  - Year-over-year comparison

- **Export Options**:
  - CSV for spreadsheet analysis
  - PDF for reporting
  - Accounting software format

### Revenue Management Workflow

**1. Revenue Recording**

The revenue tracking system allows you to document all income through:

- **Manual Entry**: Record revenue with these key fields:

  - Date
  - Amount
  - Category/Source
  - Customer
  - Description
  - Payment method
  - Project/Service association
  - Tax relevant information

- **Invoice Integration**:

  - Option to create invoice directly from revenue entry
  - Link existing invoices to revenue entries
  - Track invoice status alongside revenue

- **Recurring Revenue**: Set up recurring revenue entries for subscriptions and regular income

**2. Revenue Categorization**

Proper categorization provides clarity on income sources:

- **Category Structure**:

  - Product sales
  - Service revenue
  - Subscription revenue
  - Other income

- **Source Attribution**:
  - Customer segmentation
  - Marketing channel tracking
  - Sales representative association

**3. Invoice Management**

Streamline the billing process:

- **Invoice Creation**:

  - Generate from revenue entries
  - Customize invoice template
  - Add line items with descriptions
  - Set payment terms and due dates

- **Invoice Lifecycle**:

  - Draft → Sent → Paid/Partial/Overdue
  - Status tracking with visual indicators
  - Aging analysis (30/60/90 days)
  - Payment recording

- **Customer Communication**:
  - Email invoices directly
  - Payment reminders
  - Thank you notes for payment

**4. Revenue Analysis**

Extract actionable insights from revenue data:

- **Filtering & Search**:

  - Date range
  - Category/Source
  - Customer
  - Payment status

- **Visualizations**:

  - Revenue by source (pie/donut chart)
  - Monthly trend (line/bar chart)
  - Customer concentration
  - Year-over-year growth

- **Key Metrics**:
  - Monthly recurring revenue (MRR)
  - Average transaction value
  - Customer lifetime value (basic)
  - Days sales outstanding (DSO)
